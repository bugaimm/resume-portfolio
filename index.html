About Me

As professional writer and University of Cincinnati graduate student, my career goals center around refining my skills with each position I have the honor to work in. Whether that be in Technical Professional Communication, Marketing, or Editorial, I aim to strengthen my writing skills, explore working with a variety of genres, and find new ways to connect with diverse audiences through storytelling. Furthermore, I hope to build myself around a network of professionals in different fields, and collaborate with many to allow my work to have lasting impacts in society. Currently, I work as a graduate assistant at University of Cincinnati International in a social media and marketing role.

In my downtime, I enjoy working out—participating in various activities such as weightlifting, rock climbing, and calisthenics. Furthermore, I am drawn to the arts and enjoy crocheting, digital and traditional art, and baking when I can. Lastly, I always seeks out a book to read, which influenced my to decision to earn a BA in Creative Writing alongside my BA in Professional Writing during my undergrad years at Miami University. My leisure activities play a crucial role in my professional life, impacting the types of research I conduct, my design and content creation capabilities, and what type of work I am interested in pursuing.
